Wiz

Wiz is a very short and easy segment. Wiz has a little bit of luck involved meaning some runners will opt to do Wiz before Kracko when using this general route. You almost always want to stay in the float state unless you need to chase wiz across the screen.

Wiz with Parasol

Wiz with parasol is the easiest and most consistent way to kill Wiz early.

Hammer Mix (NOT RECCOMENDED!!)

Getting the correct timing is insanely difficult. If the Sparky is standing still, the mix setup is impossible to hit. If the sparky is doing the spark attack, getting the timing for the mix setup is a one frame window. But if the sparky is in the air from a jump, it can be a 2 or 3 frame trick.

When killing wiz with hammer, you want him to jump to the bottom left. Otherwise you will lose almost as much time as you save.
