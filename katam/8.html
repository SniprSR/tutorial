Wiz

Wiz is a very short and easy segment. Wiz has a little bit of luck involved meaning some runners will opt to do Wiz before Kracko when using this general route. You almost always want to keep floating unless you need to chase Wiz across the screen.

Wiz with Parasol

Wiz with parasol is the easiest and most consistent way to kill Wiz early. The method shown in this video comes from Pletty7 who jumps in front of the center platform after hitting wiz 4 times initially. By getting stopped by the platform you will not get hit by wiz even if he stops in the bottom center.
