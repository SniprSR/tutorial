Introduction

I'm SniprSR and this is my guide for Kirby & the Amazing Mirror any%. This tutorial is similar to swordsmankirby's Kirby 64 100% tutorial where each segment is presented in several segments as opposed to one large video for the entire game.

KatAM is a Metroidvania like Kirby game meaning you can beat the bosses in any order. Your goal in any% is to beat all 8 bosses as quickly as possible and then defeat Dark Mind, the final boss. The route used in this tutorial is a modified version of swordsmankirby's 27:48. This is the route I would recommend for beginners the most since doing segments such as Meta Knight and Moley early allows you to get a lot of the early luck out of the way. When following this general route you can do the last 3 bosses in any order. However there are many routes that can be used before introducing the Gobbler wheel mix.
