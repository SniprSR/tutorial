Kracko

Kracko is a fairly short segment that does not use much wheel at the beginning because you need burning to break the metal blocks

Shadow Kirby Luck

Shadow Kirby can give either a 1up, Tomato or Invincibility Candy. You want Candy to kill Batafire very quickly and have faster running movement speed.

If you don't get candy, then it is not the end of the world. You need to grab stone from the stone golem to kill Batafire instead. The fight is very quick, start with a dashing aerial hit into Batafire which will give you 2 hits. Then un-stone, then run towards him or stone again to get the final hit. I am unsure how much time Candy saves over killing Batafire with stone.

Advanced Kracko fight

I recommend beginners wait before going for the second double hit. One way you can save a little bit of time is by immediately going for the second double hit during kracko's attack. This is risky however since getting hit by kracko will always put you in hard knockback.
